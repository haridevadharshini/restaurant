Our Story

Founded in 2005 by Chef Rajesh Kapoor, Spice Junction brings authentic Indian flavors to Mumbai's food lovers.

What started as a small 20-seater restaurant in Colaba has now grown into one of Mumbai's most beloved dining destinations. Our chefs come from different regions of India, bringing their family recipes and culinary expertise to your plate.
